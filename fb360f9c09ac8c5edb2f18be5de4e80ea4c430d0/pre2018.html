April 17, 2019

UPDATE

Hello, me here from April 17, 2019, when I complied these entries into the master site. Doing this has me reflecting on the past and boy reading all this has been an experience. I've grown a lot since 2011. The worries and failures of the past is a key theme I realize I had. I would put down things that I worried about and also blurt out things that I was thinking about. Mostly videogames from the looks of it. There is also a clear distinction in my writing that seems to get more mature as time passes. Still a lot of these entries are very immature. The entries only go up to 2014 and a lot of 2014 isn't recorded. I would like to fill in the gaps from memory as I think this would serve future me well.

2014 iirc was a strange time. I was changed majors, moved in with Andrew and Frank, and was trying to be a better student. There was a period of great motivation where I was studying hard and working out, convinced that I had found my calling. Professor Bach/Simons really lit a fire under my ass. That lasted for one or two quarters until I started to do art stuff because polisci wasn't my favorite thing as I learned motivate can only get you so far. I learned how to cook and live by myself but summer reared it's ugly head and I fell into the heat. I did three more quarters at uci living in a new real apartment (2014) but didn't really go out much. I was living just to live. I became somewhat depressed after a terrible illness and internal conflict with myself and roomates.

2015. I had dropped out this year and was depressed and anxious about failing my parents. Summer came around and I dont remember much that happened during this. A lot of videogames I'm sure. I played a lot of Guild Wars 2. Dad cut the internet sometime before fall and I got a bit suicidal and super depressed. Literally no motivation and laid around all day. I would drink a shit ton of soda and eat very little. I think the only thing that kept me living was my buddy, Chocolate. Fast forward a few months and I frequented Starbucks searching for employment. I got a job doing manual labor for Lootcrate winter 2015. There I learned the value of a dollar and what sacrifice was.

2016. I paid for our internet and had a job so life is good right? Nope, Chocolate disappears Feb 2, 2016 and I fall into a semi-depressed state. I don't remember much, just living and working until August 2016 when PokemonGo came out. This got me out of the house rather than play gw2 all day. I actually had a pretty good rest of the summer. I remember having fun with family before I went back to school fall 2016? Learned some HTML in Nov 2016 to start this. Then came 2017 where I have summed up in an early 2018 post.
